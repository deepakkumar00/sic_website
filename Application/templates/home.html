NOTICE BOARD

Founder

Late Mr. Durga Dutt Shastri

I am from Jainti, a small village in Almora (Uttarakhand). There is limited infrastructure development in the region which often made it difficult for young men and women to get education which can provide them good life and employment. This school was established by me so that all the people of this region get a medium of education. I laid the foundation of this school in 1951, and today I am very happy that after my departure this school itself is progressing.
